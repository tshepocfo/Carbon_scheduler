Run Comparison: Savings & Emissions
Sustainability Impact Summary Test Corp is proud to report significant sustainability achievements for its 100,000 GPU hours of inference workload in the EU (Stockholm) region. Through strategic resource optimization, including the effective utilization of spot instances, we realized substantial financial savings of £36,900. This initiative also delivered a remarkable CO₂ reduction of 9,520 kg compared to baseline operations, demonstrating our commitment to environmental stewardship. Our infrastructure now operates with an impressive carbon intensity of just 24.0 gCO₂e/kWh, reflected in an optimization score of 0.79/1.0.

Crucially, these sustainability gains were achieved while maintaining optimal performance and negligible latency impact for our critical inference tasks. Test Corp remains dedicated to pioneering environmentally responsible practices, driving both ecological benefits and operational efficiency.
